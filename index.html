About My Story

Hello. I am Bella.

I like to run around and smell things. I also like to eat poop sometimes. I like to roll over. I also eat lots of yummy things. I am dog. Bella is my name. I don't have any friends because I smell bad. I'm also really mean and dusty. I bark at everyone. But I am also a philosopher. I like to think about bones and barking and growling and slobbering and dust. I also shed a lot. The place I live is covered in fur. It is everywhere. My slaves are also covered in my fur. They give me brown substance to eat. It smells yummy sometimes.

Sometimes they give me extra yummy things. But not regularly because I am a little chunky. I am also very dusty.

These are representative of how I feel on different days:

scary, sad, and all spruced up
